What is the meaning of pet services?

Pet Services means premises used to provide for the grooming of domestic animals. A veterinary hospital or a kennel is not pet services. Sample 1Sample 2. Pet Services means pet supplies, pet sales, boarding of animals, grooming and pet shop.

What is the purpose of pet care?

Pet care encompasses more than just feeding and providing shelter for our furry, feathered, or scaled friends. It's about understanding the comprehensive needs of a pet, which include their physical, emotional, and psychological well-being.
